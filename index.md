## Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a safe, affordable place to call home. Through collaborative building projects, community partnerships, and volunteer efforts, Housing Hands works to:

- Build and renovate affordable housing
- Support families in need
- Strengthen community bonds
- Advocate for housing justice
